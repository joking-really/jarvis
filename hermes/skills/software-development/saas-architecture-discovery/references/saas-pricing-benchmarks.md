## Recommended Pricing for Pre-Brand Startups

Start 30-40% below market rate. Raise after product-market fit.

### SaaS CRM (Hybrid: subscription + usage)
| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Starter | $39/user/month | Limited AI calls, basic CRM, 1-2 channels |
| Growth | $79/user/month | Unlimited AI, voice, 3 channels, pipelines |
| Pro | $149/user/month | AI builder, live monitoring, API access, white-label (logo + color) |
| Enterprise | $349+/user/month | Custom domain, full white-label, SLA |

**Final agreed pricing (user validated):**
- Starter: $39/user (down from initial $49 recommendation)
- Growth: $79/user (down from initial $99 recommendation)
- Pro: $149/user (down from initial $199 recommendation)
- Enterprise: $349+/user

**User initially thought pricing was too high.** Adjusted downward 20-30% from market rate. Pre-brand startups cannot charge Salesforce prices.

## Unit Economics

### SaaS at 100 users (Growth plan)
- Revenue: $7,900-9,900/month
- AI costs: $1,500-2,500/month
- Infrastructure: $800-1,200/month
- Gross margin: 55-65%

### Enterprise Deal ($250K/year)
- Deployment cost: $40-60K
- Ongoing costs: $5-8K/month
- Year 1 margin: 50-60%
- Year 2+ margin: 70-75%

## Key Principles

1. **Pool AI usage across organization** — reduces sticker shock vs per-user limits
2. **Annual billing with 2 months free** — improves cash flow
3. **Credits expire in 12 months** — standard, creates urgency, accounting clean
4. **Overage at 1.5x included rate** — discourages overuse without being punitive
5. **Never expire credits** — accounting liability, avoid
6. **Start low, raise after product-market fit** — user explicitly validated this approach
